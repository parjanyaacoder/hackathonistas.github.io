FAQs


What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon (also known as a hack day, hackfest or codefest) is a sprint-like event in which programmers, designers, project managers, and others collaborate intensely on software projects. The goal of a hackathon is to create functioning software based on some idea by the end of the event.


Who can attend a Hackathon?

ANYONE can attend. Yep, you read it right. Anyone who is interested in programming and loves to learn and build cool stuff can attend. If a hackathon targets a specific audience (ex, college students), then obviously only college students are allowed to attend but generally hackathons are for everyone.


Do I need Programming Experience?

You need to know the basics atleast. The organizers usually hold workshops throughout the hackathon for people who are new to programming and wish to learn more about a particular topic.
